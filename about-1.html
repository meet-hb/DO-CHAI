Vishal Pandya

Founder & CEO

Vishal was born and brought up in ahmedabad, India. His family with core values allowed him to grow up with both empathy for people and willingness to help community. During his early childhood years vishal learned a lot from his grandfather about value of strong community. His father taught him how to build strong relatinships in life. His mother devoted her time to teach him how to be humble.

Career

As young person Vishal was always willing to go extra mile to do his best, lead him to get entry into United States as young software developer. Many years of struggle, loneliness and need of survival strengthen his belief in real life values.

Growth

Vishal learn to be resilient to changes and challenges, after several years of struggle manage to reach the optimal goals living American dream.

Education

MS. In Computer Science, Champlain College, VT USA

Human Computer Interface Design: Georgia Tech, Atlanta, USA

Last Position in US: Business/Automation Strategy, Cloud consultant, Dell EMC

Mission in Life

Transforming the lives

01

Transformation

Living in America for more than 22 years, it wasn't enough of the justify the needs of his core values. He decide to move family back to India to his humble origin to find a space for him and his family with ever changing world. He also was looking for a place where he can help community and people in need.

02

Chai Nation

After several months of research, traveling across India and visiting various states it was very clear that India runs on "Chai", but Chai vendor's were still sitting at the bottom in the society, working hard everyday just to survive. As devoted chai lover, Vishal decided to pick the tab and Do Chai was born.
